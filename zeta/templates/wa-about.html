Easy insights

Let's face it: local government is impossible to follow. Washington State Congress introduced over 1400 bills and passed 685 of them in just the last year. It's a full time job to keep up! On top of that, bills are full of legalese and government jargon, making them inaccessibe to the average person. This tool will open the door for the everyday citizen to follow and weigh in on what's happening right here in Washington. A quick glance at the break down of sponsors and projected votes will equip Washingtonians to make their voice count.

Why waste time trying to sway the vote of a legislator who already has their mind set? This tool will increase the efficiency of activists, lobbyists and engaged citizens by quickly narrowing the scope of their efforts to senators and representatives who are on the fence. By pinpointing the potential sway votes, time and money are saved and impact is maximized.

Machine Learning

This tool was created using advanced machine learning algorithms. Gradient Boosted Regression proved to be the best model after exploring Logistic Regression and Random Forest.

Data

A shout out to the State of Washington which has an excellent API that provided the majority of data for "Inside the Vote". Training data consisted of voting history, bill features and legislator information from 1991 to present day.

Feature Engineering

Training data consisted of sponsors, bill topics, party, voting history. Natural Language Processing of the bill text was also incorporated.
