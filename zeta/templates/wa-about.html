Easy insights

To the everyday citizen, local goverment is increasingly opaque. One must be familiar with the legislative process, the senators and congressmen and the politics that surround particular bills. For many people this is an insurmountable barrier when it comes to understanding, following or becoming active in the local political system. This tool hopes to provide easy insights in to bills that would otherwise be a block of legal jargon. Simple information such as sponsors' parties and a prediction on vote increases accessibility.

reduced costs

Activist groups, lobbyists, companies and citizens all face the same dilemma: influencing legislators costs time and money. This tool allows interested parties to quickly identify legislators that may be swing votes so that they can limit their efforts to those senators or representatives who are likely to change their mind. Why contact someone who already has their mind made up? This allows one to cut costs by zoning in their efforts on the legislators who will make a difference.
