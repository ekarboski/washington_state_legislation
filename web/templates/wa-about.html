How it works

Easy Insights

Let's face it: local government is impossible to follow. Washington State Congress introduced over 1400 bills and passed 685 of them in just the last year. This tool gives a quick peak inside each upcoming bill, equipping Washingtonians with the knowhow they need to make their voice count.

Reduced Costs

This tool will increase the efficiency of activists, lobbyists and engaged citizens by quickly narrowing the scope of their efforts to senators and representatives who are on the fence. By pinpointing the potential swing votes, time and money are saved and impact is maximized.

Increased Engagement

The main purpose of this tool is to lessen the barrier to getting involved in local government. Data Aquisition

The data for Inside the Vote came primarily from Washington State Legislative Web Services. Over 100,000 bills and over 6,000,000 votes from 1991 to present day were used to train the machine learning algorithm.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning models were implemented to create Inside the Vote. To learn more please visit my github respository which details the feature engineering and modeling.
